RELEASES

This page is a compilation of all Berserk manga releases. It should be noted that in Berserk, traditional manga installments are episodes; i.e. the ~20 page publications commonly referred to as "chapters" in other manga are called "episode" in Berserk. Chapters are instead various segments of the story containing a range of episodes. Arcs are in turn comprised of chapters, encompassing large, specific portions of the overall story.
